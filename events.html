Host an event with us

Have an idea? Let's talk.

Schools, libraries, businesses, and community groups: if you can host a supply drive, an adoption-awareness session, or a small student volunteer event, we'd be glad to talk through it.

  • We help with student volunteers, planning, and partner intros.
  • You bring the venue, the audience, and the local connection.
  • Together we aim for something useful for the partner shelter or rescue.
