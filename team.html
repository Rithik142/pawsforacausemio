About PAWS FOR A CAUSE

Building a stronger community for animals

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is a youth-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit that connects volunteers, schools, shelters, rescues, and community organizations to strengthen animal welfare.

Get involved See our work
501(c)(3)Nonprofit · MI

Why we exist

Shelters need more than funding. They need people.

Animal shelters and rescues need dependable volunteers, community awareness, and real local relationships — not just donations. At the same time, many students and community members want to help animals but don't know where to start.

PAWS FOR A CAUSE bridges that gap. We connect volunteers and community partners with the organizations that need support, and through service, outreach, fundraising, and youth leadership we help shelters and rescues strengthen their programs and reach more of their community.

What we stand for

Our values

Eight commitments that shape how we work with animals, volunteers, partners, and the communities we serve.

01Compassion

We approach animals, volunteers, partners, and community members with empathy, patience, and respect.

02Service

We focus on practical work that addresses real needs and creates meaningful support for animal welfare organizations.

03Collaboration

We believe stronger results are possible when students, shelters, rescues, schools, businesses, and community groups work together.

04Youth Leadership

We give young people meaningful responsibilities and opportunities to organize projects, build partnerships, and lead service initiatives.

05Responsibility

We communicate clearly, protect the trust of our partners, and follow through on the commitments we make.

06Accessibility

We work to create volunteer pathways for people with different ages, schedules, skills, and levels of experience.

07Community

We build lasting relationships that help animal welfare organizations receive consistent support over time.

08Growth

We keep learning from volunteers, partners, and the communities we serve so our programs become more useful and effective.

How our model works

From a partner's need to community impact

  1. 01

    Listen to partner needs

    We start by learning what a shelter, rescue, school, or community organization actually needs.

  2. 02

    Build the opportunity

    We shape that need into a clear event, campaign, supply drive, volunteer role, or outreach initiative.

  3. 03

    Recruit & prepare volunteers

    We communicate requirements, promote the opportunity, organize signups, and help volunteers understand how to contribute.

  4. 04

    Support the work

    We assist with coordination, outreach, promotion, supplies, staffing, and other approved needs.

  5. 05

    Strengthen the relationship

    We gather feedback, improve future opportunities, and work toward consistent, long-term support.
