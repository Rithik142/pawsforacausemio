Why We Started

We’re a small, youth‑led crew that launched in July 2025 after seeing how many shelter pets slip through the cracks. Paws for a Cause exists to make animal care and adoption easier—and give teens a voice in that work.

  • Real help, fast. Adoption events, foster support, and supply drives.
  • Resources that stick. Toolkits for new pet owners and community education.
  • Powered by teens. We plan, fundraise, and partner with local shelters and clubs.
  • Growing together. Mentors & partners welcome—there’s room for everyone who cares.

What We Stand For

Compassion: We lead with empathy and care for animals and each other.
Leadership: We empower youth to make a difference in their communities.
Service: We support shelters, foster families, and new pet owners with dedication.
