The Faces Behind The Mission

Meet the team rewriting animal welfare.

From founders to chapter leads, every person here is a student volunteer, working between school, sports, and homework, to help animals that can't help themselves.

Why It Matters

Built by teens, for animals, and for the next teen who wants to lead.

We don't believe in symbolic youth representation. We believe in real campaigns, real budgets, and real responsibility. Every leader on this page runs something that ships outcomes shelters can measure.

If that sounds like the kind of team you'd want to join, get in touch. We're always looking for the next person who saw the problem, got mad, and decided to do something.
