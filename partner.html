Partner with us

Stronger support starts with collaboration

We help animal shelters, rescues, schools, community groups, and businesses extend their reach through volunteers, events, supply drives, fundraising, and outreach.

Start the conversation See our work

How we support partners

Ways we can help

01

Volunteer recruitment & staffing

We promote your opportunity, organize signups, and bring prepared volunteers to support your events and programs.

02

Event promotion & outreach

We help spread the word about events, volunteer needs, and adoptable animals across our website, social media, and school networks.

03

Supply & food drives

We organize community collections for the specific, approved items your organization needs.

04

Fundraising campaigns

We help plan and promote approved community fundraisers that bring additional resources to your programs.

05

School & youth engagement

We connect your needs with students and clubs looking for meaningful, organized service.

06

Recurring volunteer programs

We work toward consistent, ongoing support rather than one-time help.

The process

How partnership works

  1. 01

    Submit an inquiry

    Tell us about your organization and what you're working toward using the form below.

  2. 02

    Discuss your needs

    We'll talk through goals, timing, and what support would be most useful.

  3. 03

    Identify the right support

    Together we choose the volunteers, drive, event, or campaign that fits.

  4. 04

    Confirm responsibilities

    We agree on roles, requirements, and expectations for everyone involved.

  5. 05

    Promote & coordinate

    We recruit, prepare, and coordinate so the initiative runs smoothly.

  6. 06

    Review & build on it

    We gather feedback and look for ways to support you again.

Who partners with us

School partners

Community partners

Community organizations collaborating on events, education, and public outreach.

Business supporters
