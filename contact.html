Frequently Asked

Common questions, answered.

How can my school or business partner with you?+
Visit our partnerships page or message us with "Partnership" as the topic. We'll set up a 20-minute call to scope what makes sense.
Are donations tax-deductible?+
Email us for the latest on our nonprofit status and donation receipts. We're committed to transparency about how every dollar is spent.
I want to host an event in my city. How?+
Reach out via the form with "Starting a chapter" selected. We'll help you launch, playbooks, mentorship, partner intros included.
How do I drop off supplies?+
Drop-off locations rotate by drive. DM us on Instagram or email for the current list, we usually run drives quarterly.
Can younger kids volunteer?+
Absolutely. Younger volunteers are paired with age-appropriate roles like supply sorting, social media support, and event check-ins.
Do you provide volunteer hour verification?+
Yes, we issue verified hour letters for school and college applications. Just track your hours and request at the end of each month.
