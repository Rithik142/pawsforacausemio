Common questions

Before you write.

How can my school, business, or community group partner with you?+
Visit our partners page for an overview, or use this form with "Partnership question" selected. We'll follow up to set up a short conversation.
Are donations tax-deductible?+
Please email us for the most current information about our nonprofit status and whether a contribution may be tax-deductible. We try to be transparent and won't claim a tax status we don't have.
Can students under 18 volunteer?+
Yes, depending on the event and partner organization. Volunteers under 18 may need parent or guardian permission, and some partner shelters have age minimums for on-site volunteering. See our volunteer page for more.
How do I drop off supplies?+
Drop-off locations change with each drive. Please email us or DM us on Instagram for the current list. We're happy to coordinate a pickup if you have a larger donation.
Do you confirm volunteer hours for school?+
When our team or a partner organization is present, we can confirm hours for school requirements. Please let us know in advance.
