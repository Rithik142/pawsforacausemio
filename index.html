About Us

Paws for a Cause is a youth‑led nonprofit founded on July 16, 2025, by a group of high school juniors in Okemos, Michigan. We promote animal adoption, fostering, education, outreach, and fundraising for critical care—led entirely by teens. Whether hosting events or raising awareness, our mission is to improve animal lives—one paw at a time.

Our Work

We unite volunteers, businesses, and animal lovers to support shelter pets. From adoption drives to fundraising for life‑saving care, we’re building a network of support. Join us at local events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns to help give every animal a second chance.
