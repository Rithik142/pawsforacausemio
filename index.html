Our mission

How we help animals and our community

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is a youth-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit that partners with animal shelters, rescues, schools, and community organizations to promote animal welfare. We connect students and community members with meaningful volunteer opportunities, support adoption and outreach events, organize donation drives and fundraisers, and help our partners reach more people and animals in need.

Volunteer Connections

We connect students and community members with shelters and rescues that need dependable support.

Events & Outreach

We assist with adoption events, community programs, awareness campaigns, and other animal welfare activities.

Drives & Fundraising

We organize supply drives, fundraisers, and campaigns that help partners obtain the resources they need.

Youth Leadership

We give young people opportunities to lead, serve, collaborate, and create long-term community impact.
