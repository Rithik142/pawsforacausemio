Our Story

Started by teens who refused to look away.

Paws For A Cause was founded in July 2025 by three high-school juniors in Okemos, Michigan, after they saw how many animals — in shelters, on streets, in their own neighborhoods — simply needed someone to show up. So we did.

Today we're a youth-led nonprofit organizing supply drives, fostering coordination, adoption awareness, and community education — partnering with shelters and rescues across the state to make Michigan kinder, one paw at a time.

  • Real, fast help — adoption events, supply drives, and foster support that ship in weeks, not years.
  • Resources that stick — toolkits and education for new pet owners and aspiring youth advocates.
  • Powered by youth — students plan, fundraise, and partner with shelters, clubs, and local businesses.
  • Built to grow — mentors, partners, and volunteers welcome. There's a place for everyone who cares.

Our Journey

From a classroom idea to a statewide movement.

July 2025

Founded in Okemos

Three high-school juniors launch Paws For A Cause, vowing to build something teens could lead — and adults could trust.

Fall 2025

First Supply Drive

Students and local businesses come together to deliver food, toys, and bedding to Mid-Michigan shelters.

Winter 2025

Partnerships Begin

Capital Area Humane Society, Ingham County Animal Control, MSU Animal Welfare Club and more join our network.

2026 →

Going Multi-State

Expanding state chapters across Michigan, North Carolina, New Jersey, Florida, and Connecticut — with more coming soon.
