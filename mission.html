Our Mission

Hey there! We’re a group of high‑school friends who got tired of seeing pups alone on our streets—left to fend for themselves until they were gone. Watching them shuffle down sidewalks, hungry and scared, we knew something had to change.

We started small: after‑school bake sales, homemade flyers, and sharing posts online. Pretty soon neighbors, local shops, and classmates pitched in to help host our first pet adoption event.

Now, Paws for a Cause runs regular fairs, coordinates foster homes, and funds medical care for dogs in need. We shine the spotlight on pets often overlooked and help them find loving families.

We believe teens can change the world—one wagging tail at a time. Join us, and let’s make sure no dog ever feels truly alone again.
