Good to know

Donation FAQ

What's the difference between monthly and one-time?

A monthly gift is charged automatically each month and continues until you cancel — it gives us steady, predictable support. A one-time gift is charged once. You can choose either above, and enter a custom amount for both.

How do I cancel or change a monthly gift?

You can manage or cancel a recurring gift anytime through Stripe's secure donor portal (linked in your receipt emails) or by emailing pawsforacausemi@gmail.com. We'll never make you jump through hoops to stop a recurring gift.

Are donations tax-deductible?

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is recognized as tax-exempt under Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3). Contributions may be tax deductible to the extent permitted by law. Please retain your emailed receipt for your records.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes — Stripe automatically emails a receipt to the address you enter at checkout, for both one-time and monthly gifts.

Is my payment information safe?

We never collect or store card details on this website. Payment is completed on Stripe's PCI-compliant hosted page.
