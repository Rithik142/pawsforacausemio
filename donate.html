Donate or give supplies

Support a small group
doing practical local work.

Donations help us run supply drives, support partner shelters, and cover the small costs of student-led projects. We try to be transparent about everything below.

About donations and tax status. Paws For A Cause is a student-led organization. We are not currently representing ourselves as a 501(c)(3) public charity on this site, and you should not assume donations are tax-deductible. For the most current information about our nonprofit status, donation receipts, and whether a contribution may be tax-deductible, please contact us before donating.
How donations are processed. At this time, we don't accept payments directly through this website. If you'd like to support us, please use the form below or email us and we'll follow up with instructions for the type of support you'd like to give.
Ways to give

What support looks like.

There are several ways to help, including ones that don't involve money.

Supplies

Donate pet food, blankets, leashes, toys, cleaning supplies, or other items shelters need. We can share a current list of needs.

Event support

Help cover the cost of supplies, signage, refreshments, or transportation for a specific event or drive.

Shelter support

If you'd rather donate directly to a partner shelter or rescue, we're happy to point you to their donation pages. They are doing the front-line work.

Student-led projects

Support a specific student project: a school outreach session, a flyer print run, or a small supply drive in a particular community.

General support

Donate to the organization's general use. We'll apply it to whatever the partner shelters tell us is most needed.

In-kind services

Donate services like printing, design, photography, or transportation. In-kind support often makes the biggest difference for a small group.

Let us know

Tell us how you'd like to help.

Fill out the short form and our team will follow up with instructions. There is no donation processed on this page.

If you represent a partner shelter or rescue with a current need, please contact us directly and we'll match it to a drive.

We'll follow up with details within 2–3 school or business days.

Don't want to donate?

Volunteer time, share our work, or help us connect with a partner.

We're a small student-led group. Time, attention, and introductions matter just as much as funding.
