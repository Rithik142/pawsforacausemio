Support Our Mission

Your generosity helps a youth-led team empower volunteers and strengthen the animal-welfare organizations that care for animals across Michigan. Every gift supports outreach, supply drives, and community programs that make a real difference.

Why give

Where your support goes

PAWS FOR A CAUSE connects students and community members with shelters, rescues, schools, and community organizations. We do not operate a shelter or provide veterinary care — instead, your support helps fuel the volunteer coordination, supply drives, community programs, and outreach that help our partners do more.

Good to know

Donation FAQ

Can I donate online right now?

Secure online giving is being set up. Until it's live, you can email us about giving at pawsforacausemi@gmail.com, or support us by volunteering, partnering, or contributing to a supply drive.

What do donations support?

Gifts support volunteer coordination, supply drives, community programs, and outreach. PAWS FOR A CAUSE does not operate a shelter or provide veterinary care, so donations are not used for adoptions, surgeries, or shelter facilities.

Are donations tax-deductible?

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization; please consult a tax advisor regarding deductibility for your situation.

Will I receive a receipt?

When online giving is enabled, an email receipt is sent automatically by our donation provider after a successful gift. For other contributions, email us and we'll follow up.

Is my payment information safe?

We never collect card details on this website. Online payments will be processed by a trusted third-party donation provider on its own secure, hosted page.

Transparency & nonprofit information

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Michigan. We are committed to honesty about our work: we publish accurate descriptions of our programs and avoid inflated claims. Donations support general programs rather than a guaranteed allocation to specific items.

Questions about giving or how funds are used? Email pawsforacausemi@gmail.com. Please consult a tax advisor regarding the deductibility of any gift.
