FAQ Quickfire
How old do I need to be?+
Volunteers can be any age — younger volunteers are paired with age-appropriate roles like supply sorting, social media, and event support.
Do I need experience?+
No. We provide orientation and on-the-job training. Just bring willingness and reliability.
Is this remote or in-person?+
Both. Some roles (social media, design, fundraising outreach) are fully remote; events and shelter shifts are in-person.
