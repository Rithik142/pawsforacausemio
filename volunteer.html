FAQ

What to know

Opportunities vary by partner organization. Each event lists its own requirements — always review the specific event before you go.

—Who can volunteer?

Students and community members are welcome. Some events have a minimum age set by the partner organization. Group and family volunteering is often available.

—Do I need experience?

No. Most opportunities need no prior experience — just a willingness to help. Event leaders explain what to do when you arrive.

—Can students volunteer?

Yes — we're a youth-led organization. Many opportunities can count toward school or club service-hour requirements; let us know what your school needs.

—Are parents required?

When an event has an age minimum or requires guardian permission, it will be stated on the event. For some minors, a parent or guardian may need to provide consent and contact information.

—How do I find events?

Browse the events calendar for upcoming shelter visits, fundraisers, supply drives, and community events — then register using the event volunteer form above.
