Explore Our Work

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is a youth-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit. We do not operate a shelter — we connect volunteers with shelters, rescues, schools, and community organizations, and help our partners reach more people and animals through events, supply drives, fundraising, and outreach.

How We Create Impact

🤝

Volunteer Mobilization

We connect students and community members with shelters and rescues that need dependable volunteers.

🎪

Partner Event Support

We help staff and promote adoption events, vaccination clinics, and community programs.

📦

Supply & Food Drives

We organize food, toy, bedding, and essential-supply drives for partner organizations.

📣

Adoption Outreach

We help share adoptable animals, partner needs, and important animal-welfare information.

💚

Fundraising

We help raise resources for animal-welfare programs through community campaigns and collaborations.

🌱

Youth Leadership

We give young people opportunities to organize projects, lead teams, and serve their communities.

FAQs

  • How do I volunteer or host an event? Fill out our volunteer or contact form and our team will follow up with next steps.
  • What age do I need to be? It depends on the event and partner organization. Many opportunities welcome students; some events have a minimum age, and minors may need parent or guardian permission.
  • Does PAWS FOR A CAUSE run a shelter? No. We are a nonprofit that connects volunteers and supports the shelters, rescues, and organizations that care for animals.
