Our work

How We Support Animal Welfare

PAWS FOR A CAUSE is a youth-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit. We don't run a shelter — we strengthen the ones that do. We connect volunteers with shelters, rescues, schools, and community organizations, and help our partners reach more people and animals through events, supply drives, fundraising, and outreach.

What we do

Practical ways we help partners

01

Volunteer mobilization

We connect students and community members with shelters, rescues, and programs that need dependable help, and make each opportunity clear with schedules, roles, age requirements, and preparation.

02

Event support

We recruit volunteers and provide support for adoption events, community programs, shelter activities, clinics, and outreach.

03

Supply & food drives

We organize community collections for the specific, approved items partner organizations request.

04

Outreach, awareness & fundraising

We raise visibility for adoptable animals and partner needs, and promote approved community fundraisers that bring in additional resources.

05

Youth leadership & lasting partnerships

We give students real responsibility and build recurring relationships that support partners over the long term.
