FAQs

Quick answers, before you ask.

How do I host an event or volunteer?+
Fill out our volunteer form or contact us. A coordinator will follow up within 48 hours.
What age do I need to be?+
Volunteers can be any age — we have age-appropriate roles for everyone, from social media support to in-person event work.
Where can I drop off supplies?+
Drop-off points rotate by drive. DM us on Instagram or email pawsforacausemi@gmail.com for current locations.
