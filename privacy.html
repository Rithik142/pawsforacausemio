Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information. Last updated 2026.

What we collect

When you sign up to volunteer, donate, or contact us, we collect basic info like name, email, and the message you send. That's it. We don't track you across the web.

How we use it

We use your info to respond to your message, route you to the right campaign, and (only if you opt in) send you our newsletter. We don't sell, rent, or trade your data — ever.

Cookies

This site uses minimal technical cookies for basic functionality. We don't use third-party advertising or behavioral tracking.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time, and email pawsforacausemi@gmail.com to request deletion of any data we hold on you.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at pawsforacausemi@gmail.com.
